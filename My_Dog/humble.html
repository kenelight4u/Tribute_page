Humble Beginnings

Reminiscent of another great spiritual leader, Mollie started her life in a barn. As a runt competing with seven siblings for Puppy Chow and pig scraps, she learned early on to assert herself. She claimed what was hers without apology, but her authority was tempered with an innate kindness and patience. She was a firm believer in non-violence.

It was perhaps this serene sureness that drew her first followers to her. Claire Moore and Marsha Morgan loved the little scrapper at first site. It was the Mohawk cowlick that captured their attention, but Mollie's depth of soul that captured their hearts.

Mollie and her moms were meant to be together.

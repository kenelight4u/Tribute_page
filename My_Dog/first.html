First Mate

More than two women's best friend, Mollie was an active partner in the adventurous lives of Claire Moore and Marsha Morgan. She accompanied them on almost every journey they ever took. Road trips in their car or RV, hikes in national parks, walks on the beach, winters in San Diego, but her favorite adventures were onboard the Makai, Claire and Marsha's Nordic tugboat.

Mollie and her women nearly lived on the sea for three boating seasons, and Mollie proved herself to be a first-rate seadog. She could navigate the outer deck like a mountain goat and control her bladder for 12 hours. Every cove, dock, and port brought a new adventure, which inevitably included swimming, hiking, and, of course, sticks. Mollie was tireless when it came to outdoor activities, but in the confines of the boat, she was calm and quiet, with patience befitting a yogi.

Nose to the air and stern to the sea, Mollie Llama was surely at her happiest.
